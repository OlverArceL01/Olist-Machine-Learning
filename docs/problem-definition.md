# Problem Definition

Although we do not have access to [Olist's](https://olist.com) internal business metrics. the company operates at a large scale (+50k customers and thousands of daily sales).
At this level, deriving insights manually becomes increasingly difficult, especially for tasks such as demand forecasting or customer segmentation.

Using the publicly available Olist dataset, this project focuses on exploring two common machine learning problems in e-commerce:

## Demand Forecasting

1. Based on historical sales data, how much demand can we expect for each product in the coming weeks or months?

## Customer Segmentation

2. What types of customers can be identified according to their purchasing behavior?
